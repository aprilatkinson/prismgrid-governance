# How PrismGrid Fits Into a Modern Marketing Stack

PrismGrid is not another tool competing for ownership of execution.

It is the **governance layer** that makes modern marketing stacks safe to scale.

Most enterprises already have more tools than they can manage.
The problem is not capability.
The problem is **coordination under acceleration**.

---

## The Reality of Modern Marketing Stacks

A typical enterprise marketing stack includes:

- CMS and ecommerce platforms
- Paid media and experimentation tools
- Competitive intelligence platforms
- Review and sentiment analysis tools
- Agencies and external partners
- Increasingly: AI chat tools, agents, and workflows

Each system is useful.
None of them is accountable for **truth consistency across the system**.

This is where breakdowns occur.

---

## The Core Failure Mode: Local Optimization

Every tool optimizes locally:

- CRO tools optimize conversion
- Ad platforms optimize click-through
- Agents optimize output velocity
- Teams optimize their own KPIs
- Agencies optimize deliverables

No system is responsible for asking:

> “Are we scaling something the market actually believes?”

Local optimization without a shared reality produces **global contradiction**.
